# BriefLink launch announcement

## Primary launch post

Client work gets messy fast.

A call ends. Feedback lands in Slack. Decisions sit in email. Someone adds notes to a doc. Then the team has to reconstruct what the client actually asked for and what happens next.

BriefLink is built for agencies and consultancies that need a cleaner way to turn scattered client conversations, requests, and decisions into a clear, shareable brief.

We’re starting with teams that sell and deliver custom client work, where clarity matters because every handoff, revision loop, and follow-up affects delivery quality.

If your team has ever lost time translating client input into something actionable, BriefLink is for you.
