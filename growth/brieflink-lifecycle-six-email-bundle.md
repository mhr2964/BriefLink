# BriefLink Lifecycle Six-Email Bundle

## Usage notes
This bundle contains the full lifecycle email set for BriefLink across two target segments:
- Agencies: Emails 1–3
- Consultancies: Emails 1–3

All emails follow the standing CTA standard:
- Use `Get early access` for all primary pre-signup conversion CTAs
- Reserve `You’re on the list` for post-signup confirmation states only

All emails use the approved BriefLink wedge:
- calm client prep
- risk visibility
- `account risk` terminology
- preferred phrasing around preparing `before client calls`
- required positioning line: `Prepare with a clear brief.`
