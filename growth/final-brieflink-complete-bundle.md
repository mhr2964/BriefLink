## 1) Landing Page Copy

### Hero
**Eyebrow:** BriefLink for agencies and consultancies

**Headline:** Prepare for client calls with a clear brief and earlier visibility into account risk

**Subhead:** BriefLink helps client-service teams get aligned before client calls, surface risks sooner, and walk into every conversation with a calmer, clearer plan.

**Primary CTA:** Get early access

**Supporting line:** Built for agency and consultancy teams managing active client relationships.

### Problem
**Headline:** Client prep gets fragile when context is scattered

**Body:** When meeting prep is split across inboxes, chat threads, notes, and last-minute pings, teams go into client conversations with avoidable uncertainty. Important details get missed, ownership stays fuzzy, and small gaps become account risk.

### Value
**Headline:** A calmer prep workflow before client calls

**Body:** BriefLink gives agencies and consultancies one place to prepare with a clear brief, align on what matters most, and catch account risk before the meeting starts.

**Bullets:**
- Pull the latest context into one working brief
- Surface open questions before they become client-facing
- Reduce avoidable account risk
- Help account leaders walk into meetings better prepared

### How it works
**Headline:** Prepare with a clear brief.

**Step 1:** Gather the latest client context, notes, and action items.

**Step 2:** Turn scattered inputs into a brief the team can align around.

**Step 3:** Review gaps, talking points, and account risk before client calls.

### Audience
**Headline:** Built for client-service teams

**Body:** BriefLink is for agency owners, principals, account directors, client partners, and consultancy client-service leads who need stronger prep across live client relationships.

### Closing CTA
**Headline:** Better prep leads to better client conversations

**Body:** If your team wants calmer prep, clearer briefs, and earlier visibility into account risk, BriefLink is built for that workflow.

**Primary CTA:** Get early access
