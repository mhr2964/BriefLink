# BriefLink First Source Bundle

## Usage notes
This document is the first bundled source handoff for BriefLink growth assets. It contains finalized source materials for the landing page, waitlist/signup flow, and founder outreach. Apply `Get early access` as the only primary conversion CTA across all pre-signup assets in this bundle.

## Locked messaging baseline
- Audience: agency owners, agency principals, account directors, client partners, and consultancy client-service leads
- Positioning: calm client prep and risk visibility for client-facing teams
- Core promise: help teams prepare for client conversations with a clearer brief and earlier visibility into account risk
- Required terminology:
  - `Get early access` for all pre-signup primary CTAs
  - `account risk`
  - `before client calls`
  - `Prepare with a clear brief.`
- Prohibited for pre-signup conversion contexts:
  - `request early access`

## Landing page source copy

### Hero
**Eyebrow:** BriefLink for agencies and consultancies

**Headline:** Prepare for client calls with a clear brief and earlier visibility into account risk

**Subhead:** BriefLink helps client-service teams get aligned before client calls, spot gaps that create account risk, and walk into every conversation with a calmer, clearer plan.

**Primary CTA:** Get early access

**Secondary supporting line:** Built for agency and consultancy teams that manage live client relationships.

### Problem section
**Section headline:** Client prep breaks down in the last mile

**Body:** When notes, actions, and context live in too many places, teams head into client conversations carrying unnecessary uncertainty. Important details get buried, next steps stay fuzzy, and small misses turn into account risk.

### Value section
**Section headline:** A calmer way to prepare before client calls

**Body:** BriefLink gives account and client-service teams one place to pull together the working brief before client calls. Instead of scrambling through documents and chat threads, your team can see the essentials, align on risks, and show up prepared.

**Bullets:**
- Pull the latest context into one clear working brief
- Surface open questions and client-sensitive gaps early
- Reduce avoidable account risk before meetings happen
- Help every client lead walk in with a steadier point of view

### How it works
**Section headline:** Prepare with a clear brief.

**Step 1:** Gather the current client context, notes, and open threads.

**Step 2:** Turn scattered inputs into a brief your team can actually use.

**Step 3:** Review risks, gaps, and next talking points before the meeting starts.

### Who it’s for
**Section headline:** Designed for client-service teams

**Body:** BriefLink is built for agencies and consultancies that need better prep across account leadership, client partners, and delivery teams. It is especially useful when multiple people contribute to a client conversation but one person has to lead it confidently.

### Closing CTA
**Section headline:** Walk into client conversations better prepared

**Body:** If your team wants more consistent prep, clearer briefs, and better visibility into account risk, BriefLink is built for that workflow.

**Primary CTA:** Get early access
